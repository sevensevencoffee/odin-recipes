Tom Cruise Cake

Prep Time: 20 mins Cook Time: 1 hr 25 mins

Description

This “Tom Cruise Cake,” inspired by the decadent dessert the movie star famously sends as a holiday gift, is as irresistible as it gets. The buttery sour cream pound cake features white chocolate chips and sweetened coconut flakes and is topped with a cream cheese frosting — what’s not to love?

Ingredients

  • all-purpose flour
  • salt
  • Baking Powder
  • White Sugar

Instructions

  1. Finely chop the white chocolate if you don’t want caramelized chunks of chocolate in the cake.
  2. Double the coconut extract if you want a stronger coconut flavor.
